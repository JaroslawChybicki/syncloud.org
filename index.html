Run your services at your premises

1. Get Hardware


Syncloud device is a small box with low power consumption. Standard device is built on Raspberry Pi 2 Model B.

Buy it!    or build yourself

2. Connect Everything


Connect your device to your home router. Use one of the available LAN ports. Plug in power wire and turn the device on.

3. Activate


Use Syncloud Android application to activate your device, link it with domain name at syncloud.it and enable ownCloud. Watch video for this step.

4. Use It!


Now you ready to use your online storage! Access. Sync. Share. Learn more about ownCloud features.


Download ownCloud client application for your platform:

Windows Mac OS X Linux Android iPhone
